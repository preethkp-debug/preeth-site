Case 001 · 2025

North Pine SaaS

A B2B compliance SaaS stuck at $2M ARR with flat acquisition. We rebuilt the content engine from scratch around programmatic SEO and an AI-assisted editorial pipeline.

Programmatic SEO AI Editorial B2B SaaS
North
Pine

The brief

Three years of category content had stopped compounding. Every new article cannibalised an old one. The team was tired of writing.

What we did

Built a topical map of 1,400 cluster pages. Designed an AI editorial pipeline — keyword cluster → brief → first draft → human review → publish — that brought time-per-article down from 9 hours to 90 minutes. Internal-linked the entire site at the cluster level. Killed 280 pages that were dragging the rest of the site down.

The outcome

Twelve months later, organic traffic was up 412%, qualified pipeline was up 6×, and the editorial team was producing more content than they had been hiring three freelancers to write.

Case 003 · 2025

Mintlayer Studio

The brief

"We're profitable but exhausted. Every retainer is structured so growth means more headcount. We can't scale this business."

What we did

Mapped every recurring deliverable — content briefs, social posts, ad scripts, blog QA, reporting — and identified eighteen places where an AI workflow could replace either a human task or a hand-off.
