FAQ

Things people always ask.

Do you work with agencies or only direct?+
Both — but no white-label. If I'm working with your agency, my name is in the room and on the deck. I think it's better for everyone.
What size of company do you typically work with?+
Most of my clients are between $1M and $50M ARR (or DTC equivalent). I've also worked with one or two larger enterprises through private engagements. Pre-revenue is rare — I'm rarely the right fit there.
How do you price?+
Audits are fixed-fee. Build engagements are project-based with a clear scope and a single success criterion. Fractional retainers are monthly. I don't do hourly — it's a bad incentive system for both of us.
Can you guarantee results?+
No, and you should run from anyone who does. I will guarantee the work, the cadence, the documentation, and the honesty. The numbers are a function of a hundred variables, only some of which are mine to control.
Do you sign NDAs?+
Yes. Mutual NDAs are standard before we get into anything substantive. Send yours or use mine.
Where are you based / what time zones do you work in?+
Bengaluru (IST). I've structured my week to overlap with US East Coast mornings and UK afternoons. Most clients find it works in their favour — the work compounds while they sleep.

Thirty minutes. Tell me what you're trying to do. I'll tell you whether I'm the right person — and if I'm not, I'll usually know who is.
